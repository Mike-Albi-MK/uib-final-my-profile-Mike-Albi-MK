ABOUT ME

My Location

Gazing at the 'Fernsehturm', window shopping at the Kudamm or immersing yourself in the true meaning of the word 'Kiez'. Yes, I live in Berlin and love it. It's truly a city where you can feel and meet various cultures and personalities once you step outside. I know it all, I haven't seen everything. And you can learn more about Berlin on this site.

My Profession

Staring at the desktop, moving pixels until dawn. Who cares about regular working hours? Nah, it is not always that way. But web development keeps you on your toes. There is a new riddle to be cracked for every customized solution. And you definitely want to be someone who likes to feel the heartbeat of the ever-evolving 'flux'.

My Passion

How do I detach myself from work? How do I maintain my relationship with the real world? I literally strike a chord on my guitar. It can be challenging and comforting at the same time. I still want to learn to become an uber-shredder but it is also rewarding to just play around with simple chords and melodies.
